Guess the mystery fighter

  • You are trying to guess a fighter who is currently on the UFC roster
  • Enter the name of the fighter that you wish to guess
  • Green in any column indicates a match
  • Yellow in the Fighter Reach or Fighter Height column indicates that you are within 2 inches of the fighters reach or height
  • Yellow in the age column indicates that you are within 2 years of the fighters age
